The Starmer-Burnham leadership contest and the Makefield by-election were covered on all outlets including the partial clips from Sky News and GB News. Channel 4 News was alone among fully-analysed outlets in treating the Scottish by-elections in Arbroath and Aberdeen South as a discrete story (5.1 mins).
